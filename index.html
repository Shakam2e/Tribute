Fanice Murila

1957-2017

The Late

Fanice Murila (15 October 1957-27 jUly 2017) was a hardworking and humble woman who was well educated and intelligent. She envisioned her dreams set outt to accomplish them . She was also a mother who deeply cared about her children. She was a nurse by profession and an entrepreneur. She invested in poultry farming,real estate and bonds. She was successful at most of her dreams.
